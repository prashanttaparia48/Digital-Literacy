 # Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist

 ## Do’s

- Think before posting—ensure your content is respectful and appropriate
- Use privacy settings to protect personal information
- Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation
- Communicate politely and professionally
- Respect others’ opinions and engage in healthy discussions

 ## Don’ts

- Don’t share sensitive personal or academic information
- Don’t engage in cyberbullying or offensive behavior
- Don’t post inappropriate or unprofessional content
- Don’t spread rumors or unverified news
- Don’t use social media excessively during study or work time
