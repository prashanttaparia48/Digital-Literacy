# Stay Safe Online – UPI & Cyber Safety Checklist

## General Safety Tips
- Never share your OTP, passwords, or UPI PIN with anyone
- Do not click on suspicious links from unknown sources
- Always verify the identity of callers claiming to be from banks
- Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication
- Avoid public Wi-Fi for financial transactions
- Keep your phone and apps updated

## UPI / Financial Safety Tips
- You do not need to enter your UPI PIN to receive money
- Never scan unknown QR codes to receive payments
- Always check payment requests before approving

## Reporting Cybercrime
- Report at: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Helpline: 1930
- Inform your bank immediately in case of fraud
